<em>Primes</em> is in <em>P</em>

August 11, 2002

This is seriously elegant: Apparently, three Indian mathematicians have shown that you can test primality (deterministically) in polynomial time. That's incredible. More incredible, I seem to understand the paper. (The authors seem proud as punch, as you'd expect, cracking a centuries old problem.)
